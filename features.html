Key Features

Designed to Enhance Knowledge Transfer

Turnover is equipped with a suite of features aimed at minimizing knowledge loss during employee transitions and maximizing efficiency in knowledge transfer.

  • Comprehensive Knowledge Base: Centralize your company’s knowledge, making it easily accessible to new hires.
  • Interactive Training Modules: Engage new employees with interactive training sessions that speed up the learning process.
  • Expertise Tracking: Track and manage the distribution of expertise across your company to identify knowledge gaps.
  • Feedback and Improvement System: Continuous improvement through feedback from users to enhance the platform’s effectiveness.
  • Collaborative Tools: Facilitate collaboration between employees for better knowledge transfer and retention.

With these features, Turnover aims to transform the way companies handle job transitions, making the process as seamless and efficient as possible.
