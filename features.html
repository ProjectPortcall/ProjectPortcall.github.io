Key Features

Empowering Seamless Transitions with AI

Turnover leverages the power of OpenAI to offer a unique set of features designed to minimize knowledge loss and streamline the job transition process.

  • Multi-user ChatGPT Interface: Facilitates collaborative conversations between multiple users and the AI, allowing for seamless knowledge transfer and interaction in real-time.
  • Contextual Knowledge Integration: Integrates organizational documents and expertise directly into the chat, enabling the AI to provide context-specific assistance and information.
  • Dynamic Knowledge Base: Documents and conversations are continuously incorporated into the system, enhancing the AI’s understanding and the overall knowledge base for future references.
  • Streamlined Onboarding and Training: Provides a centralized platform for new hires to learn and adapt to their roles efficiently, significantly reducing the time required for training.

With Turnover, organizations can ensure a more effective and efficient turnover process, making every transition smoother and more knowledgeable than the last.
