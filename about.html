About Turnover

Our Mission

Turnover's mission is to streamline the transition phase for companies facing job turnovers, ensuring that the knowledge and expertise remain within the company. By facilitating an efficient knowledge transfer process, we aim to minimize the operational disruptions caused by employee transitions.

Our Vision

We envision a workplace where knowledge transfer is seamless and intuitive, enabling businesses to maintain continuity and growth regardless of staffing changes. Turnover is committed to becoming the leading solution for knowledge retention and transfer in the modern workplace.

Our History

Founded by a team of HR and technology experts, Turnover was born out of the need to address the recurring challenge of knowledge loss during employee transitions. With years of combined experience, we've developed a platform that not only retains but amplifies enterprise expertise over time.
